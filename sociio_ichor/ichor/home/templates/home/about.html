About us

There has always been neglect in blood donation, causing a shortage in every locality, leading to gazillion lives being lost every day. We aim to instil blood donation into the lifestyle of every healthy individual in India and believe that any life can be saved if connected with the right person at the right time. This can also solve the vital issue of the supply-demand gap and prevent blood scarcity.

Every day 12,000 people die due to blood shortage.

Innumerous myths surrounding blood donation.

Gazillion untargeted messages on social media.

Roughly 4100 units of blood get wasted everyday.

With the second wave of COVID-19 hitting India hard, there have been many difficulties in reaching out to people. It has become difficult to treat the patients who tested positive with COVID-19 due to the lack of plasma donations. The communication gap between the patients and the donors has become increasingly problematic, which we at Sociio, under the project ICHOR, intend to improve. We hope to be able to connect the struggling patients with compatible donors and bring the rising state of Covid under control.
