About Us

Hoping to bring a change in this world, we aim to connect the donors and patients across all of India through this cause. Ever since the advent of Covid-19, there has been discernible neglect towards blood donation causing a shortage in every locality. We aim to instil blood donation into the lifestyle of every healthy individual in the country. We believe that every life can be saved if connected with the right person at the right time. No person in this world deserves to lose a life due to inefficient storage of blood units. By bringing a balance to these numbers on the basis of demand by embedding people’s lifestyle with blood donation, and providing the right connections of donors on time is the key to solving this issue.

Every day 12,000 people die due to blood shortage.

Innumerous myths surrounding blood donation.

Gazillion untargeted messages on social media.

Roughly 4100 units of blood get wasted everyday.

Our Story
300+ Donors

10 Team Members

It started with the need for platelets by one of our teammate’s volunteers. His sister was pregnant and was in dire need of platelets. Even after searching everywhere, it led to no avail. After learning of their situation by the time our teammate was able to get his compatibility test done, the patient had passed away with their newborn. This stirred up a whirlpool of emotions among us and led to the beginning of Sociio Ichor.

With the second wave of COVID-19 hitting India hard, there's been much difficulty in reaching out to people. It has become difficult to treat the positive COVID-19 patients due to lack of plasma donations. Communication gap between the patients and the donors has become problematic, which we at Sociio, under project ICHOR, intend to improve. We hope to make it easier in bringing the patients as well as the donor in connecting and bringing the rising state of Covid under control.
